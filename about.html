Our Story

LockGram was born from a simple observation: social media platforms have become surveillance machines that profit from user data while offering little value back to the people who create the content.

Founded in 2024 by a team of privacy advocates, Web3 developers, and social media experts, LockGram set out to build something different‚Äîa platform where:

  • Privacy isn't optional: End-to-end encryption is built into every feature
  • Users own their data: Web3 technology gives users true digital sovereignty
  • Creators are rewarded: The LockCoin system fairly compensates meaningful engagement
  • Communities thrive: Decentralized governance puts power in users' hands

Today, we're building the infrastructure for a more equitable internet‚Äîone where technology serves people, not the other way around.
