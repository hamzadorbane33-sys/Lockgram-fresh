How LockGram Protects Your Privacy

A step-by-step look at our security process

1

Device-Level Encryption

When you send a message, it's encrypted on your device before transmission using AES-256 encryption with your unique keys.

2

Secure Transmission

Encrypted data is transmitted using TLS 1.3, protecting against man-in-the-middle attacks during transit.

3

Encrypted Storage

Data is stored in encrypted form on our servers. Even we cannot read your message content without your encryption keys.

4

Recipient Decryption

Only the intended recipient can decrypt the message using their private keys, ensuring true end-to-end security.
