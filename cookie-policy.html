1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how LockGram ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and platform. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which provides additional information about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

At LockGram, we believe in minimal data collection and maximum privacy. Our approach to cookies reflects this philosophy by using only essential cookies necessary for platform functionality.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies contain information that is transferred to your device's hard drive. They do not contain any information that identifies you personally and cannot harm your device.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

LockGram uses a minimal set of essential cookies only. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies that collect personal information.

10. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please be aware that some platform features may not work properly:

  • Performance: Some features may load more slowly

Essential cookies required for basic platform functionality cannot be disabled without affecting your ability to use LockGram.
