Frequently Asked Questions Find Answers

Everything you need to know about LockGram, our Web3 social platform, LockCoin rewards, and privacy features.

What is LockGram?

+

LockGram is a privacy-first Web3 social platform that combines traditional social networking with cryptocurrency rewards. We offer end-to-end encryption, AI-powered features, and a fair reward system where users earn LockCoins for meaningful engagement and quality content creation.

How is LockGram different from other social media platforms?

+

LockGram differs in several key ways:

  • Privacy-first: End-to-end encryption and zero-knowledge architecture
  • User rewards: Earn LockCoins for genuine engagement and content creation
  • Web3 integration: True ownership of your data and digital assets
  • No tracking: No ads, trackers, or data selling
  • Fair compensation: Creators and users get rewarded for their contributions

Is LockGram free to use?

+

Yes! LockGram is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees, premium tiers, or hidden costs. You can start earning LockCoins immediately after joining, and all core features are available to everyone.

What platforms will LockGram be available on?

+

LockGram will be available on multiple platforms:

  • Android: Native Android app (Early Access coming soon)
  • iOS: Native iPhone & iPad app (Coming Q2 2025)
  • Web: Browser-based platform
  • Desktop: Desktop apps for Windows, Mac, and Linux (Coming Q3 2025)

What is LockCoin?

+

LockCoin is our digital reward token that users can earn through platform engagement. It represents value within the LockGram ecosystem and can be used for various platform features and interactions.

How do I earn LockCoins?

+

You can earn LockCoins through:

  • Creating content: Post quality content that engages the community
  • Engaging with others: Meaningful interactions with other users' posts
  • Referrals: Invite friends to join LockGram
  • Community participation: Active, positive presence in the community

Do LockCoins have real monetary value?

+

LockCoins are designed for use within the LockGram ecosystem. While they represent value and can be used for platform features, their primary purpose is to reward meaningful engagement and contributions to the community. LockCoin values and utility may evolve as the platform develops.

Do I need a crypto wallet to use LockGram?

+

Not immediately! You can start using LockGram and earning LockCoins without a wallet. However, to access certain advanced features or withdraw LockCoins, you may need to connect a crypto wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet. We support all major wallets.

Can I transfer LockCoins to other users?

+

LockCoin transfer functionality is part of our roadmap. Currently, LockCoins are earned through engagement and can be used within the platform. Future updates will include enhanced transfer and utility features.

How secure is LockGram?

+

LockGram implements industry-leading security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption: AES-256 encryption for all messages
  • Zero-knowledge architecture: We cannot access your message content
  • Perfect forward secrecy: Each conversation uses unique encryption keys
  • Regular security audits: Third-party security assessments
  • Open source: Transparent codebase for security review

What data does LockGram collect?

+

We follow a minimal data collection approach:

  • Account data: Email, username (required)
  • Content data: Your posts and messages (encrypted)
  • Usage data: Basic interaction data for platform improvement
  • Device data: Technical information for security and optimization

We never sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties without your consent.

Can LockGram read my messages?

+

No. LockGram uses end-to-end encryption, which means messages are encrypted on your device before being sent and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient. Even we cannot read your message content.

Is my location tracked?

+

No, LockGram does not track your location. We don't use GPS, IP-based location services, or any location tracking mechanisms. Your privacy is protected by design.

How can I delete my account and data?

+

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the account settings. This includes:

  • Deleting all your posts and content
  • Removing your account information
  • Clearing any cached data

Contact [email protected] if you need assistance with account deletion.

We're exploring ways to help users connect with their existing networks. This may include:

Privacy and user consent will always be our top priority.

+

LockGram is built on modern, secure technology:

+

+

LockGram is designed to be data-efficient:

Can I use LockGram offline?

+

Limited offline functionality is planned:

  • Message drafts: Compose messages offline

How do I create an account?

+

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Visit our website or download the app
  2. Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
  3. Provide your email address
  4. Choose a unique username
  5. Create a secure password
  6. Verify your email address
  7. Start using LockGram!

Can I change my username?

+

Yes, you can change your username, but there are some considerations:

  • Username changes may be limited to prevent abuse
  • Previous usernames may become available to others
  • Some integrations may be affected by username changes

We recommend choosing your username carefully during registration.

How do I reset my password?

+

If you forget your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Can I have multiple accounts?

+

We recommend having one account per person for the best experience. Multiple accounts may be limited to prevent abuse and ensure fair distribution of LockCoin rewards. If you need multiple accounts for legitimate purposes, please contact our support team.

How do I delete my account?

+

To delete your account:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Settings/Account
  3. Find "Delete Account" option
  4. Confirm your decision
  5. Your account and data will be permanently deleted

This action cannot be undone. Contact support if you need assistance.
